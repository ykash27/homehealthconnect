Security & Compliance

HIPAA Built In.
Not Bolted On.

Every layer of HomeHealth Connect is designed for HIPAA compliance from day one — from the database to push notifications.

Business Associate Agreement (BAA)

Signed at account creation on every plan. Your agency is covered before the first patient record is entered. No negotiation, no extra cost.

End-to-End Encryption

All PHI encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Patient files in the document directory are encrypted in Supabase Storage. No exceptions.

Role-Based Access Control

Patients see only their own data. Clinicians see only their caseload. Admins see only their agency. Enforced at the database level — not just the application.

Full Audit Logging

Every login, data access, record creation, update, and deletion is timestamped and logged with user ID — ready for compliance review at any time.

Notification Safety

Push and email alerts never contain PHI. Notifications say "You have a new message" — never the content itself. HIPAA-safe communication by design.

Agency Privacy Isolation

Agencies cannot see each other's patients, clinicians, or data — even if a clinician works for multiple agencies. Isolation enforced at Supabase Row Level Security.
